Highlight Update 4.6

Arlecchino and Lyney Banners The Version 4.6 Banners for Phase 1 feature Arlecchino's banner release, alongside Lyney's Banner Rerun! Both banners are available from April 24, 2024, to May 14, 2024! The featured 4-stars in the Phase 1 Banners of Version 4.6 are Xiangling, Lynette, and Freminet, and are available for the same duration as Arlecchino and Lyney!

Wanderer and Baizhu Banner Rerun. On the other hand, the Version 4.6 Banners for Phase 2 feature Wanderer's and Baizhu's banner reruns! Their banners are slated to run from May 14, 2024 until June 4, 2024!

The Phase 1 weapon banner will feature Crimson Moon’s Semblance (Polearm) and The First Great Magic (Bow). The phase 2 weapon banner should feature Tulaytullah’s Remembrance (Catalyst) and Jadefall’s Splendor (Catalyst). The 4-star weapons for the first phase are Favonius Warbow, Eye of Perception, Portable Power Saw, Dragon’s Bane, and The Dockhand’s Assistant. The 2nd phase 4-star weapons are yet to be revealed.
